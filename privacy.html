Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2024

1. Overview

Safe Surf ("we," "us," or "our") is a Chrome extension designed to help users understand the privacy implications of websites they visit by analyzing privacy policies and terms of service pages. This Privacy Policy explains how Safe Surf handles data, user privacy, third-party services, and user rights in accordance with applicable laws including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) in Canada.

2. Data We Collect

5. User Rights

Access Rights

Since we don't store personal data, there's no need to request access to your data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Right to Erasure

Simply uninstall the extension to remove all local data. No server-side data deletion is necessary as we don't store any user data.

Control & Settings

Customize the extension's behavior through the settings panel, including AI analysis preferences and notification options.

6. Children's Privacy

Safe Surf is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children
  • Parents should supervise children's online activities
  • Contact us immediately if you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child
